Who We Are

Nearly three decades of hope for South India's children.

Udhaya — United Human Action for Young Associates — is a charitable trust founded in 1997. For close to thirty years we have stood with vulnerable children and families through education, care, health awareness, and community development — welcoming every child regardless of caste, creed, religion, gender, or background.

We provide a safe, family-style home where children receive shelter, schooling, healthcare, and the room they need to grow with dignity and purpose.

More about us

Our Vision

A society where every child can access education, healthcare, and protection — and grow to their full potential with dignity, hope, and purpose.

Our Mission

To empower children and families through education, awareness, healthcare, and community support that builds lasting, sustainable change.

Our Divisions

Three pillars of hope and transformation.

Udhaya Children's Home

A safe, family-style home — shelter, nutritious meals, schooling, and medical care for children who need it most.
