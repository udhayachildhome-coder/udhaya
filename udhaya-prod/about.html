About Udhaya

United Human Action for Young Associates — a charitable trust giving hope to South India's children and families since 1997.

Who We Are

Nearly three decades of standing with children who need it most.

Udhaya (United Human Action for Young Associates) is a charitable trust established in 1997 in South India, with a mission to support vulnerable children and families through education, care, health awareness, and community development.

For close to thirty years, we have served underprivileged and marginalized children regardless of caste, creed, religion, gender, or social background — because every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and live with dignity.

Today, Udhaya offers a safe, family-style environment with educational support, shelter, healthcare, and personal-development opportunities. Our work is made possible by the sponsors, donors, and volunteers who share our vision of lasting change.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A society where every child has access to education, healthcare, and protection — and the opportunity to achieve their full potential while living with dignity, hope, and purpose.

Our Mission

To empower children and families through education, awareness, healthcare, and community-support initiatives that promote sustainable development and social well-being.

Our Work

What We Do

Education & Child Development

Quality education, scholarships, study centers, and vocational training — including support for school dropouts and out-of-school children.

Child Welfare & Protection

Child-rights awareness and safe, nurturing environments — standing against child labor, abuse, and discrimination in all forms.

Health & Hygiene

Awareness on hygiene, nutrition, and preventive care, plus medical assistance and healthcare access for deserving children and families.

Family & Community Development

Counseling and guidance, help accessing government welfare programs, and community campaigns for cleaner, safer neighborhoods.

Collaboration & Advocacy

Partnering with organizations near and far, publishing awareness materials, and advocating for equity, justice, and the dignity of every person.

One mission, many hands.

Every program above runs on the generosity of sponsors and volunteers who believe no child should be left behind.
