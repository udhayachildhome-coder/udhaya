Every Child Has a Dream. Together, We Help Them Achieve It.

Every child deserves a safe home, quality education, loving care, and the opportunity to build a brighter future. At Udhaya, we nurture each child's potential in a family environment where they can learn, grow, and succeed.

A Day in Their World

Life at Udhaya

From the first prayer of the morning to family time at night, each day is full of learning, play, and togetherness.

  1. 6:00 AM

    Morning Prayer

    The day begins together with a quiet moment of gratitude and reflection.

  2. 7:00 AM

    Breakfast

    A warm, nutritious meal to start the day with energy.

  3. 8:30 AM

    School

    Off to class, the heart of every child's journey forward.

  4. 3:30 PM

    Homework

    Guided help to finish assignments and build steady habits.

  5. 5:00 PM

    Study Time

    Focused learning and tuition support with caring mentors.

  6. 6:00 PM

    Sports

    Running, games, and teamwork out in the fresh air.

  7. 7:00 PM

    Music

    Singing and instruments, joy that lifts every spirit.

  8. 7:30 PM

    Evening Prayer

    A gentle close to the day's activities, together as one family.

  9. 8:00 PM

    Dinner

    Everyone gathers around the table to share the day.

  10. 8:30 PM

    Family Time

    Stories, laughter, and rest, the warmth of belonging.
